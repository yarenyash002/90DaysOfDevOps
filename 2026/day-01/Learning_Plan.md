# 90-Day DevOps & Cloud Learning Plan

**What is your understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?**  
DevOps is a combination of Development and Operations teams working together to deliver applications faster, making them more reliable and easier to manage.  
- Automate repetitive and manual tasks  
- Deliver software faster and more efficiently to end users

- I want to take the 90-day challenge and stay consistent throughout
